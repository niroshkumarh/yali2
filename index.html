Team Sea Sakthi is excited to participate in the prestigious Monaco Energy Boat Challenge 2023 as the official team representing our institution. Our team comprises of 10 passionate students who are dedicated to developing a unique hybrid boat concept. Our boat will combine hydrogen fuel cell, batteries, and solar panels, showcasing innovative technological solutions that align with the sustainability of the marine sector. We look forward to presenting our concept to the judges and competing against some of the brightest minds in marine propulsion. Join us on our journey to the Monaco Energy Boat Challenge 2023 and support our commitment to a sustainable future!

  • Indigenous propulsion system
  • Hydrogen fuel cell technology
  • Solar panel integration
  • Carbon-fiber reinforced body

As a team, we are committed to promoting sustainable marine solutions and pushing the boundaries of technology in the maritime sector. Participating this event allows us to build on our experience and knowledge, refine our designs, and showcase our latest innovations including indigenous propulsion system and hydrogen fuel cell in the Monaco Energy Boat Challenge.

Monaco Energy Boat Challenge 2023 happens from 3rd July to 9th July 2023.

There are three classes:
  Energy class,
  Solar class,
  Open Sea class.

We, Team Sea Sakthi, are proud to announce that we secured the 6th global position and won the communication prize of 2000 euros at the Monaco Energy Boat Challenge.

News Update

The boat, designed by a 10-member student team called Team Sea Sakthi, is set to race at the 10th edition of the Monaco Energy Boat Challenge in july.Last year built an electric catamaran which finished sixth in the global competition. There will be 17 competitions from across the globe, including two professional teams this year. Team Sea Sakthi will be the only team from India to participate in the event, principal of the college D Saravanan said.

A group of 10 engineering students from Kumara Guru College of Engineering in Tamil Nadu's Coimbatore have developed a hydrogen-powered energy boat, which is the first of its kind in India. This is the only team from India which will be taking part in the Monaco Energy Boat challenge to be held in Monaco during the first week of July. The boat and its propulsion were indigenously designed by the students and will compete against international boat racers from across the world.
